# Player Tier Fame ↕ Merit ↕ Power ↕ Rewards
How Distribution Works ▼

Player Tiers are assigned by fame earned in the event. Players with higher fame qualify for P-Tier 1 and receive priority access to top-tier rewards. Players with lower fame are assigned P-Tier 2 and still receive meaningful rewards.

Reward Tiers indicate item rarity and value:

T1 — Premium T2 — Valuable T3 — Common T4 — Filler

Category Limit: Each player can receive a maximum of 5 reward categories per round. Resources (Oil, Food, Odinium) each count as 1 category. This prevents any single player from monopolising the treasury.

CP-Aware Allocation: The system tracks each player's combat power and adjusts rewards accordingly (less likely to need mastery pages, as an example), and algorithm works to ensure rewards are not over-weighted to higher CP players. Players who contributed more receive higher-value reward tiers, while still ensuring every eligible player receives something.

Cross-Round Balancing: Reward distributions are context-aware — each round considers cumulative rewards received thus far to ensure fair distribution. Players who received fewer or lower-tier rewards in previous rounds get priority in the current round. Treasury inventory resets each round (undistributed items are lost), so the algorithm maximizes usage of all T1 and T2 rewards every round.

Transparency: All distributions are recorded here exactly as executed. Player names are shown as they appear in-game. UIDs are never stored or displayed.
