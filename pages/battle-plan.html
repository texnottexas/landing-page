What To Do read before you engage
Skim through the plan targets below
Know the tiers and which wastelands are priorities before you head in. Don't waste time figuring it out once Contest opens.
Check chat for target call-outs
When you log in, first thing: check alliance chat or world chat for targets from trusted players / leaders only. Please do not share random other wastelands in chat during the event.
First login bug — click the Wasteland button twice
On first login, the Wasteland button is bugged. The first click takes you to an assigned wasteland (wrong target). Go back to chat, click the link again, and the second click lands you on the correct wasteland.
Only attack targets you can actually kill
This is a war of attrition — every wasted attack is a heart lost on our side. Do NOT use Rockfield. Use your strongest march to kill anyone you can. If you can't kill anyone at that wasteland, tell a leader and we'll hand you another target. If you have even 10 seconds to wait, just wait and check chat — leaders will call out when we're ahead and give the next target. Every wasted attack adds up.
Ask a leader if you're unsure
Alliance chat is fine for quick questions. Better to ask than waste an attack.

Rules of Engagement read before contest
Smalls EMPTY when defending

If your small ships have marches, enemy gets bonus attacks against your Mothership. Always put all marches in the Mothership.

Attacks are per-player, per-day

You get about 10 attacks total (up to 18 vs enemies with smalls up). Not per wasteland. Spend them on top-priority targets only.

Win = more hearts at 90 min

Or empty their hearts first. For PvE you can win on garrison alone if you flood enough players there.

PIVOT fast on PvE

Once our heart count is ahead of the PvE Mothership, stop attacking that wasteland. Move to the next PvE or a Tier A target.
