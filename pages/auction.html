Auction House / Merchant Guild

2864 hub

Auction House is a periodic server event where commanders list Titan Gear with Special Effects or Legendary Random Attributes for bidding using Auction Coins. The 2026 revamp adds the Merchant Guild Gifts layer with Mega-Discount items, Merchant Convoy restocks, and trivia.

This page aggregates mechanics and reward data extracted from the live client — all localization text is the game's own English.

Core loop

  • List: Commanders above a minimum VIP level can list Titan Gear with Legendary SE, Epic SE, Rare SE, or Legendary Random Attributes. Items can also be listed anonymously. There is a per-player listing cap.
  • Bid: Other players pay Auction Coins to bid. Each bid must be at least the current price plus the step (defined per-auction in the table).
  • Revenue: When a listing is sold, the seller receives Revenue Coins (sale price minus tax).
  • Auto-bid: Commanders can set a max bid — the system auto-increments by the minimum step when outbid, until the max is reached, the item is sold, or the bid is manually terminated.
  • Red envelope: When the highest bid for a listing exceeds a trigger threshold, a server-wide celebration red packet drops for all players.
  • Leftovers: Unused Auction Coins persist across events and can be exchanged for items in the Gift Shop after the event ends.

What can be sold

  • Legendary SE (Special Effect titan gear)
  • Epic SE
  • Rare SE
  • Legendary RA (Legendary Random Attributes)
  • Titan Runes — one rune may be listed at a time
Listing rules: Titan Gear must not be equipped, must not be locked, and must have saved refining results. Once a bid has been placed on your listing, you cannot delist it. Anonymity can be toggled at listing time (often cannot be canceled later).

Bidding modes

  • One-time bid: Place a single bid at your chosen amount.
  • Auto-bid: Bid once at the minimum increment, then automatically re-bid by the step amount whenever outbid, until the listing ends, you are at your maximum, or you manually terminate.
  • Requirement: you must have increased a bid at least twice before the auto-bid feature unlocks for that listing.
  • Outbid refund: When outbid, your previous Auction Coins are automatically refunded.

System messages

    How they trigger

    • When any player's winning bid on a single item exceeds the trigger threshold, a Celebration Red Packet is distributed server-wide.
    • Each server envelope has a capped participation limit; xingjun (rank) and total participants caps apply.
    • Reward types: quality-3 packets contain the standard reward, and a shared jackpot can drop for one lucky winner.

    Exchange leftover Auction Coins

    After the event ends, remaining Auction Coins can be exchanged for items in the Gift Shop. Each item has a personal limit per cycle plus a server-wide cap. Prices shown are the true (discounted) Auction-Coin cost; the original price is crossed out.

    Merchant Guild Gifts — 2026 expansion

    • Merchant Convoy restocks four times per period (server time 04:00, 10:00, 16:00, 22:00).
    • Rankings: top-5 commanders by Auction Coins spent each period unlock Mega Discount items for the entire warzone in the next Merchant Guild event.
    • Privileges: winning auctions earns extra Mega-Discount Attempts, which bypass the server-wide cap.
    • Showroom: items auctioned above a threshold appear in a public showroom; the winning bidder triggers a World-Map bonus gift for the entire warzone.
    • Discount Store: two tiers — Mega Discount (limited, rush-based) and Standard Discount.
