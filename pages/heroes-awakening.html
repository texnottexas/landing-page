For whale-y players

Be careful when trading your active secondary marches in each unit type. The Phase 2 and Phase 3 awakening heroes are not expected for several months. During this time you will lose the ability to use those heroes in other marches.

TW will not have another hero swap event after this window. So you will need to sacrifice those heroes to loan to Rivergame until they finish developing the hero system and awakening features, at which time there is no solid release date.

For some of you this may be a significant impact to your ability to defend and attack buildings. For others, less or no impact. This information is provided so that you may make an informed decision about how to best use the hero swap, as you will be giving up an ES7 and a hero to just sit idly on one of the 11 heroes you may already own.

What is this?

No more "new hero every month."

The game now has 18 final heroes. Three unit types. Six combo trios. Hero Swap lets you trade in any old hero you've leveled and move all that progress onto one of the 18.

How a swap works in 4 steps

1
Pick a target
One of the 18 new heroes. Same unit type as the hero you trade in.
2
Pick a source
An older hero you no longer need. They get reset.
3
Resources move
Hero Exclusive Skills, Hero Shards, and Hero XP from the source land on the target.
4
Done, locked
That target is used up. Nobody else can swap to that hero again.

The rules to know before you swap

Allowed

  • Army hero to Army target
  • Air hero to Air target
  • Navy hero to Navy target
  • Hero must be in Base, not on a march

Not allowed

  • Mixing unit types
  • Swapping to a target someone already swapped to
  • Undoing a swap. It's permanent.

What moves to the target

Hero Exclusive Skills
Both equipped and in your bag
Hero Shards
Everything spent to 5-star the source hero
Hero XP
All the XP you spent leveling the source
Source hero
Resets to 1 star, Lv. 1 with 10 Shards

Watch out: Talents (Perks) swap too

The source hero and target hero swap their perks. If you want to save the perks for the target hero, please transfer them either onto the source hero or to another hero, or they may be lost.

Tip: Use Exchange Talent in-game to move perks before you swap.

Who can I swap to? The 18 targets.

Each army type has 6 targets grouped into 2 trios of 3. When all 3 trio heroes are in the same march, a trio bonus kicks in.

What is awakening?

A new way to level up your best heroes. Each awakened hero gets up to +240,000 Combat Power. Right now only 6 Phase I heroes can be awakened.

You'll spend two different things

Awakening uses two separate currencies. Don't mix them up.

Awakening Stones
9,000 per hero

What it does: Levels up the 4 awakening skills (Tier 1 to 10).

Where to get it: Path of Awakening event, Grand Slam vouchers, mail.

Key milestone: 675 stones to push one skill from Tier 1 to Tier 6 (the big payoff).

Hero Shards + Lv. 1 Hero Skills
1,140 + 740 per hero

What it does: Raises that hero's Awakening Level from 1 to 40 (which unlocks the 4 skills).

Where to get it: Same Hero Shards and Lv. 1 Hero Exclusive Skills you already use for that hero. Lv. 1 to 9 of the meter needs only Hero Shards. Lv. 10 onward also needs Lv. 1 Hero Skills (each Lv. 1 skill is crafted from 25 Exclusive Skill Shards, so 740 chips = 18,500 shards if you have to craft them).

Key milestone: Awakening Lv. 30 unlocks Skill 4 (all 4 open).

Beyond Tier 10? There's more progression past Tier 10 on each skill (more sub-levels using the same Awakening Stones, around 39,900 stones per hero for the full extension). Most players will be far from Tier 10 for a long time. Worry about this later.

The simple plan

Pick one march. Push one hero at a time. Get each skill to Tier 6 before starting the next one.

  1. Pick one trio in your strongest unit type.
  2. Awaken those 3 heroes and push each to Awakening Lv. 30.
  3. Take Skill 1 to Tier 6, then Skill 2, then 3, then 4.
  4. Only after all that, worry about Lv. 40 or ES7.

The big questions

Stones and Hero Exclusive Skills are expensive. Here's how to spend them wisely.

When should I stop pushing Awakening Level?

Stop at Awakening Lv. 30.

Why? Lv. 30 unlocks all 4 skills. After that, every level costs 50 Hero Shards plus 40 Hero Exclusive Skills for only +2,000 Combat Power. Those Exclusive Skills are better spent elsewhere.

Lv. 30 is the sweet spot. Come back for Lv. 30 to 40 only after Tier 6 on all 4 skills.

ES5 or ES7?

Start with ES5. Save ES7 for later.

ES5 needs 81 Hero Exclusive Skills. ES7 needs 729. That's 9 times more.

Those same Hero Exclusive Skills also fuel Awakening Lv. 10 to 40. The extra 648 you'd spend on ES7 could pay for 20+ Awakening Levels instead. ES7 boosts every awakening skill's buff, but only after those skills exist.

ES5 first, then awakening, then ES7 once your skills are at Tier 6.

One strong march or many small ones?

One focused march wins.

A fully awakened hero gives +240,000 Combat Power. A whole march of them adds up to +1.2 million Combat Power.

The combo trio buff only activates when all 3 trio heroes are in the same march together. Spread them out and the bonus disappears.

Backup marches stay strong without awakening. Pour your resources into one trio.

What order should I do this in?

For each hero you pick, follow these steps in order:

  1. Get them to 5 stars, Lv. 120, ES5
  2. Use 1 Awakening Card to awaken
  3. Push Awakening Level to 30 (all 4 skills unlock)
  4. Skill 1 to Tier 6, 675 stones
  5. Skill 2 to Tier 6, 675 stones
  6. Skill 3 to Tier 6, 675 stones
  7. Skill 4 to Tier 6, 675 stones
  8. Later: ES7, Tier 10s, Lv. 40, sub-track grind
Don't spread tiers thinly across all 4 skills. Finish one skill to Tier 6 before starting the next.

Awakening Level 0 to 40

The 40-level meter is fueled by 2 per-hero items.

  • Hero Shards — the same shards you used to summon and 5-star this hero. Spent on every level 1 to 40.
  • Lv. 1 Hero Exclusive Skills — the same Lv. 1 skill chips you use to level this hero's Exclusive Skill. Spent on levels 10 to 40 only. Each Lv. 1 chip is crafted from 25 Exclusive Skill Shards, so the table below also shows what that would cost in shards if you have to craft them.
