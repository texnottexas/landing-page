Awakening Skill Loadouts live

Each awakened hero has 4 stackable Awakening Skills + an ultimate "kill name" skill. Per-skill icons extracted directly from the patch resources bundle. Buff system — per-row 521xxx family live

Each awakening level grants 4 buffs: 3 per-row variants (front / mid / back) from one specialty family, plus 1 global buff for the hero's army type. Hero scaling caps at +900 per row + a +3,600 global buff at the 40-level peak.

Awakening Level Cost — full 40-level ladder live

Each level needs 100 EXP, gained at 20 EXP per pull → 5 pulls per level. Gold Shard and ES Skill columns are per-hero items — each hero uses their own Hero Shard and a dedicated Exclusive Skill chip (see the per-hero list below). Per-pull cost is shown with the per-level total (×5) in parentheses. Fight Power is the cumulative awakened power at that level.

Cost tiers visible at a glance: Lv.1–9 = 2|0, Lv.10–19 = 4|2, Lv.20–29 = 6|4, Lv.30–40 = 10|8 (Gold Shard | ES Skill per pull). To max one hero from 0 → 40, you need roughly 1,000 Hero Shards + 700 ES Skill chips of that hero.

Per-hero awakening items live

Each of the 18 awakening heroes uses two existing items — their Hero Shard and a dedicated ES Skill chip — to climb the 40-level ladder. Max level for every hero is 40.

How awakening progression works live

Hero level — 40 ranks

Each awakened hero has 40 levels of awakening progression. Each level grants ~2000 power (capping at 80,000 power total). Item cost ramps: 2|0 shards/level for Lv.1–9, 4|2 for Lv.10–19, up to 10|8 for Lv.30–40.

Skill level — 10 ranks each

Each of the 4 awakening skills has its own Lv.1–10 ladder. Every level applies a required buff to your hero, and reaching Exclusive Skill Lv.7 upgrades that buff to a more potent variant — so ES7 heroes get the most out of awakening.

Late-game progression

Beyond the visible 10 skill levels, awakening continues into hundreds of sub-tiers per skill (18 heroes × 4 skills × 100 sub-levels). Each sub-tier applies two army-type-specific buffs (Army/Navy/Air Force) and adds power to your awakened total.

Awakening Choice Chest cadence

Each Phase delivers 1 chest immediately, then 5 more on a 7-day cadence — 6 total per phase. Backlog accumulates: if you miss a week, you'll still receive every chest you were owed when you log back in.
