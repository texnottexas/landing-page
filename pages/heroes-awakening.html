Heroes Awakening & Heroes Exchanging

The new hero system — reconciled from in-game tables and Rivergame Discord posts

Announced 2026-04-24 · live data 2026-04-29

How this page is organised guide

Top War's New Hero System bundles two features. Heroes Awakening is a universal upgrade path for any 5★ Lv.120 hero. Heroes Exchanging is a 7-day event that lets you convert any of 24 'Pool' heroes into any of 18 'Core Lineup' heroes (same army type). Below: mechanics, the Pool ↔ Core calculator, every hero spotlight Rivergame published, plus what was already loaded into the client that they didn't talk about.

Tags [discord] = sourced from Rivergame Discord posts. [game] = decoded from the live game client (often unreleased).

Heroes Awakening — mechanics discord

Unlock + flow

Hero must reach 5 Stars and Level 120. Tap [Hero] → [Awakening] tab to begin.

Each Awakened hero unlocks 4 new Awakening Skills, promoted separately. Each tier (Tier 1 → 4 visible in the preview UI) increases the Awakening Stats Bonus.

Source: Discord 2026-04-24 announcement, Discord 2026-01-08 teaser.

Awakening Choice Chests

Free from the event interface. 1 chest drops immediately at event open; more after timed intervals; 6 total per batch of Awakening Heroes. Forgotten chests accumulate — next collection grants the full backlog.

Source: Discord 2026-04-24 announcement.

Bonus formula

Increases active skill damage by N%, plus enemies with abnormal status take X% extra damage. The bonus stacks with the hero's existing Exclusive Skill levels.

Why this exists

The "one new hero per month" treadmill ended in January 2026. Awakening keeps trained main heroes relevant — no more constant power-creep replacement.

Source: Discord 2026-01-08 ("New Hero System Coming Soon!").

Heroes Exchanging — Pool ↔ Core calculator discord

Pick a donor from the 24-hero Exchange Pool (left). Eligible Core Lineup targets (right) auto-filter to the same army type. Star level + Exclusive Skill levels on the donor are inherited 1:1 by the target. Excess materials are not wasted. Exchange itself is free.
