What this report does: Cross-references R12.5 deployment counts (from the in-game War Declaration Info panel) against current buff-cap state and which wastelands unlock Level 2 / Level 3 Neutral City declarations next round.

Player → march conversion: Panel shows player count out of 200, but each player has 2 marches. Marches shown here = panel-num × 2.

NC declaration cap (this round only): Next wasteland round we can declare on at most 1 L1 + 1 L2 + 1 L3 NC. Unlocking multiple L3 NCs is redundant for declarations (only useful as insurance).

Owned NC protection check: Any wasteland that, if lost or not won, opens a 1-2 hop threat path to one of our owned L2/L3 NCs is marked PROTECTS Lx NC #N and kept regardless of buff cap. Losing a protector means the enemy can declare on our NC next round.

Recommendation key: REALLOCATE — buff is fully capped, no L2/L3 NC unlock, and not a protector. CONSIDER — buff near cap, low NC value, not a protector. KEEP — strategic, buff has headroom, or NC protector.
