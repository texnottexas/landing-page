How To Do Rockfield Like A Pro

1

Heroes: The Cast

  • Rockfield — The hero of our story!
  • Kuruzo — Boosts your March Size by 5*
  • Requires Kuruzo at 5*, otherwise use any 5* SSR powerhouse of your choice.
2

Skills: March Size Matters

  • Rockfield Exclusive Skill
  • March Size (Rare)
  • March Size (Normal)
3

Units: Air Force ONLY

  • Best optimal performance: Valhallas (level 103), or use +2 unit cards from backpack. The effect is the same, without Odinium cost, and can use them in your base defense to send them to repair bay first.
  • Not recommended: Heavy Troopers
  • True damage is reduced if your units are lower level than the enemy.
4

Maths: For the Geeks

Air Force Pre-Battle Skill:

  • Trigger: Activates once before the first battle round
  • Effect: Deals (X)% of each enemy unit's max HP as true damage

Damage Reductions:

  • −50% damage per level your units are below enemy's average level
  • −1% damage for every 1% fewer troops than the enemy

Bonus: Inflicts (X)% Debilitation on all enemy Naval units for 2 turns

* (X varies by player)

5

Field Tests

  • Air Force vs. Army: Effective
  • Air Force vs Naval: Effective
  • Air Force vs Air Force: Not Effective
