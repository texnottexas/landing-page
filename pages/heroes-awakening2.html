What is Awakening?

A new progression layer on top of 5★ Lv. 120 heroes. Awaken a hero with their Awakening Card to unlock a new persona, 4 stackable Awakening Skills, an ultimate kill-name skill, and the post-cap Honor Wall — for a total of up to +240,000 Fight Power per hero. Awakening rolls out in 3 phases of 6 heroes each.

Audit fixes vs production
  • Skill stone cost ladder — production says "2–10 per level", real cost is 15/15/30/30/45/45/60/80/130 per pull × 5 pulls = 75/75/150/150/225/225/300/400/650 per level.
  • Per-hero max — production never surfaces this. Real: 9,000 stones to max all 4 skills. 54,000 for Phase I.
  • Awakening Level cumulative shards — production says 1,090 + 700, real 1,140 + 740 (Tier 4 covers Lv. 30–40, 11 levels, not 10).
  • Max FP per hero — production says +80,000; real +240,000 (80k ladder + 120k skills + 40k Honor Wall).
  • ES7 buff IDs — production says 21x101 → 21x1101; real is 7-digit IDs where digit 3 flips 0→1 (e.g. Sara skill 1 2201101 → 2211101).
  • Honor Wall — not on production at all. Added as its own section with calculator.
  • Item naming — production switches between "Gold Shard" and "Hero Shard". Game lang only uses Hero Shard (e.g. "Sara Shard", "Marvingt Shard").

Awakening Level 0 → 40 — Hero Shards + Exclusive Skill chips per hero

Each level needs 100 EXP, gained 20 per pull → 5 pulls per level. Per-pull cost is tier-based: Lv. 1–9 = 2 shards, Lv. 10–19 = 4 shards + 2 chips, Lv. 20–29 = 6 + 4, Lv. 30–39 = 10 + 8. Hero Shards and ES chips are per-hero items (e.g. "Sara Shard", "Sara Skill chip").

After a skill hits Lv. 10, the Honor Wall opens. Each of the 4 skills has 2 sub-tracks of 200 sub-levels each. Costs ramp in tiers of 50, then 80, 120, 150; free at 200.

Hero Swap — donor → target by army type #

Three pools, one per army type. 8 donors and 6 awakening targets per pool. Targets are grouped into 2 combo trios per pool — when you deploy all 3 members of a trio together, the boost listed on the trio card activates.

Hero Swap rules #

What transfers

  • Hero level + star count + Exclusive Skill level fully convert to the target hero.
  • Hero Shards above 10 transfer 1-for-1 in the target's Shard.
  • Talents inherit bidirectionally — both heroes pick up each other's talents (untransferred talents reset, materials not refunded).

What you give up

  • Donor is reset to 1★ Lv. 1 with 10 base Shards retained — you keep access to them.
  • Heroes must be in Base (not on march, not on task) to swap.
  • Each target hero can only be swapped TO once.
  • Same army type only: Army↔Army, Navy↔Navy, Air↔Air.
