3 When the Capital opens

♚ Hold the Capital — in this order

  1. Capital first. Mass onto it and keep continuous control — 100 pts/min is the game.
  2. Skeleton crews stay. Leave a few on each Lv.2 ruin and the Lv.3 ruin so we don't bleed those points while everyone chases the Capital.
  3. Rotating squad on the Lv.1 ruins. A small floating group recaptures Lv.1s as they flip — cheap points, low commitment.
  4. Defense holds the line. Tex & Peewee keep our home Capital locked so we win the tiebreaker if scores are close.

5 Things to remember

  • Capture = 60s. Hold continuous control for 60 seconds to claim a building.
  • Then 120s safe. After you capture, it's protected for 2 minutes — can't be attacked.
  • 5-min switch lock. Jumping between the two battlefields locks you out for 5 minutes — pick your map and commit.
  • No shields inside. Shields drop on transit and can't be turned on in the battlefield.
  • 50K+ merit fights. That's the eligibility bar for both our squad and the enemy.
  • Tiebreaker = their Capital. Equal score? Whoever holds the enemy Capital wins — then enemy ruins, then top-10 personal score.
