How To Do Rockfield Like A Pro

1

Heroes: The Cast

  • Rockfield — The hero of our story!
  • Kuruzo — Boosts your March Size by 5*
  • Requires Kuruzo at 5*, otherwise use any 5* SSR powerhouse of your choice.
2

Skills: March Size Matters

  • Rockfield Exclusive Skill
  • March Size (Rare)
  • March Size (Normal)
3

Units: Air Force ONLY

  • Best optimal performance: Valhallas (level 103), or use +2 unit cards from backpack. The effect is the same, without Odinium cost, and can use them in your base defense to send them to repair bay first.
  • Not recommended: Heavy Troopers
  • True damage is reduced if your units are lower level than the enemy.
4

Maths: For the Geeks

Air Force Pre-Battle Skill:

  • Trigger: Activates once before the first battle round
  • Effect: Deals (X)% of each enemy unit's max HP as true damage

Damage Reductions:

  • −50% damage per level your units are below enemy's average level
  • −1% damage for every 1% fewer troops than the enemy

Bonus: Inflicts (X)% Debilitation on all enemy Naval units for 2 turns

* (X varies by player)

5

Field Tests

  • Air Force vs. Army: Effective
  • Air Force vs Naval: Effective
  • Air Force vs Air Force: Not Effective

March Setup

Kartz Trial is great for titan gear materials, catalysts, and both individual / alliance rewards.

To maximize our server, alliance, and your individual score, here are some tips:

General

  • BALANCE your buffs, grasshopper. You are not some bro just working on your arms at the gym — 2 offense buffs, 2 defense buffs, balance.
  • Use your hits. Do not let the attempts go over 29/30.

Heavy Troopers

  • Use all of your Heavy Troopers (HTs) in ONE march.
  • Put chips on all HTs — a gold core + 6 white chips gives a lot of stats.

General Tips

  • Performance scales with troop power — the higher level your units are, the higher your score will be.
  • A base full of Valhalla units or level 102/103 units will perform better than half a base of level 102 units.
  • Make sure all of your heroes in base defense setup, under more options, have skills assigned and titan gear.

Advanced Tips

After rounds where your primary HT dies and you are using a garrison march, replace the HT in your base defense.

For players with multiple unit type marches, one method to improve your score is to remove your garrison setup completely. Then between rounds, go into base defense setup, click on more options, then toggle the order of the marches to counter the upcoming waves.
