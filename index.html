  • Effect: Deals (X)% of each enemy unit's max HP as true damage

  • −35% damage per level your units are below enemy's average level
  • −1% damage for every 1% fewer troops than the enemy

Bonus: Inflicts (X)% Debilitation on all enemy Naval units for 2 turns

General Tips

  • Performance scales with troop power — the higher level your units are, the higher your score will be.
  • A base full of Valhalla units or level 102/103 units will perform better than half a base of level 102 units.
  • Make sure all of your heroes in base defense setup, under more options, have skills assigned and titan gear.

Advanced Tips

After rounds where your primary HT dies and you are using a garrison march, replace the HT in your base defense.

For players with multiple unit type marches, one method to improve your score is to remove your garrison setup completely. Then between rounds, go into base defense setup, click on more options, then toggle the order of the marches to counter the upcoming waves.
