        Wasteland Conquest — How to Play

        Stages

        1
        Publicity Stage — War declarations are placed on enemy/unclaimed wastelands. No fighting yet.
        2
        Contest Stage — All declared battles activate simultaneously. Players march to attack or defend.
        3
        Settlement Stage — Battles resolve, ownership updates, scores calculated.

        Defense Rules

        🛡
        Each player can be assigned to defend one wasteland. They do not march — they fight from within the wasteland.
        ⚔
        Defenders still have their remaining marches available to attack other wastelands.
        !
        A wasteland with no assigned defenders is contested only by the occupying server's general strength. Assign defenders to critical wastelands.

        Attack Rules

        🚀
        Attacking players march to the target wasteland during the Contest Stage window.
        #
        Each player has 2 marches total (across all battles). A player defending uses 0 marches for defense but can still send 2 attack marches.

        March Limits Per Wasteland

        Each wasteland has 3 ships. A player can only be in 1 wasteland but places their 2 marches across ships within it.

        ShipMax MarchesHearts/PlayerNotes

        Total max: 200 marches (100 players × 2 marches each). Put your primary march in Mothership, secondary in a small ship to keep both alive for the extra-attack benefit.

        Buff Priority

        ★
        ATK Buff (Lv3 = +270% ATK) — highest combat priority, defend at all costs.
        ★
        DMG Increase (Lv3 = +45%) and DEF Buff — high value, prioritize defense.
        ★
        DMG Reduction (Lv3 = -45% DMG taken) — defensive multiplier, important during large battles.
        ○
        Economy buffs (Truck Transport, Mining Hub, etc.) are lower priority for combat assignment.
